Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

We collect personal information necessary to operate the marketplace, including your name, email address, and M-Pesa phone number. Your M-Pesa number is used strictly for STK push payments and B2C withdrawals.

Your data is used to secure your account, process payments, and verify your identity to prevent fraud on the platform. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Payment transactions are encrypted and processed through verified APIs (PayHero/Safaricom).

4. Contact

For data removal requests or privacy concerns, please contact us via our official WhatsApp support channel.
