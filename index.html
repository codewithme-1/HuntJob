Common Questions

Everything you need to know about the market.

How does HuntJob protect my payments?
Every task listed on our market is pre-funded. The client's money is held securely in escrow. When your submitted work is approved, the funds are instantly released to your withdrawable balance. You never work for free.
How fast are withdrawals processed?
Instant. We use the M-Pesa STK push and B2C API. Once you hit the withdraw button on your dashboard, the funds hit your registered mobile wallet in seconds. No waiting 14 days like other platforms.
Do I have to pay to access jobs?
Creating an account is free. To bid on tasks, you use Tokens. You can activate specific Hunter Tiers inside the dashboard to unlock free daily tokens, lower competition, and access to premium, high-budget technical contracts.
Where do these jobs come from?
We bring the market to you. Our platform aggregates high-demand requests from global freelance sites, local agencies, and direct B2B startups, centralizing them onto one easy-to-use Kenyan platform.
